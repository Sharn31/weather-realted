ABOUT THE WEATHER DISEASE PREDICTOR

Exploring how climate shapes disease patterns Harnessing data to predict future outbreaks Advancing global health and sustainability.

What Is Our Project About?

Our project aims to predict and analyze how climatic factors such as temperature, humidity, and rainfall contribute to the spread of infectious diseases. By combining machine learning with environmental data, we strive to identify early warning signs that can help reduce health risks and improve sustainability.

Why We Chose This Project

Climate change is one of the most pressing challenges of our time. It not only affects ecosystems and economies but also human health. Rising global temperatures and changing weather patterns have led to the emergence and spread of new diseases. Our mission is to leverage technology to analyze and forecast these risks.

Contributing to Global Sustainable Development

SDG 3: Good Health & Well-Being

We focus on improving health outcomes by preventing climate-related diseases through data-driven insights.

SDG 13: Climate Action

Our research promotes awareness and proactive strategies to combat the health impacts of climate change.
